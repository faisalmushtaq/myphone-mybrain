Taking part

Designed to work with school life.

{{ site.data.content.process_intro | markdownify }}
01

School conversation

We discuss practical fit, timings, year groups, safeguarding routes and the information schools need before deciding whether to take part.

02

Information and consent

Parents, carers and pupils receive clear study information before any research activity begins. Only pupils with the right permissions take part.

03

School-based research session

Research sessions may include questionnaires, cognitive tasks, EEG recording and optional smartphone data donation, delivered by trained researchers.

04

Feedback and learning

We work with schools to share accessible findings, support learning about research and contribute evidence that can help future decision-making.
